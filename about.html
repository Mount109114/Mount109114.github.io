About Me

I am S. H. M. Muntasir Rahi, a master's student in Mechanical Engineering at Clarkson University and a Graduate Teaching Assistant in the Department of Mechanical and Aerospace Engineering.

Originally from Bangladesh, I completed my undergraduate degree in Mechanical Engineering at BUET.
