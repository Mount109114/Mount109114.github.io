Projects & Research

A selected overview of my research and engineering work, combining experimental investigation, computational modeling, and practical system development.

Current Research

PIML Ultrasonic Framework

Developing a physics-informed machine learning framework for inverse ultrasonic characterization of granular and viscoelastic material systems. This work combines forward physical modeling with data-driven estimation of material behavior.

Current Research

Moisture-Induced Tablet Study

Investigating how moisture changes pharmaceutical tablet microstructure and viscoelastic properties using non-destructive ultrasonic methods, including time-of-flight, attenuation, and dispersion analysis.

NDT

Ultrasonic Defect Detection in Metal

Experimental investigation of embedded crack detection in mild steel using ultrasonic methods, with calibration-based analysis and defect sizing through amplitude comparison techniques.
