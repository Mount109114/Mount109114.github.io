Lorenz Attractor

An interactive 3D butterfly-effect plot with a dark premium style, live controls, and full camera interaction. Drag to rotate, scroll to zoom, and explore the attractor from any angle.

Controls

Adjust the visible range and Lorenz parameters. The plot updates live.

Equations dx/dt = σ(y − x)
dy/dt = x(ρ − z) − y
dz/dt = xy − βz
Interaction Rotate with drag
Zoom with wheel
Pan with right drag

Nonlinear
